The Grammar of Graphics

## Aesthetics
(chapter 10)

* Form (position, size)
* Surface (color, texture)
* Motion (direction, speed)
* Sound (tone, volume)
* Text

"Sound" is part of the grammar even
though we might not see it graphically.
